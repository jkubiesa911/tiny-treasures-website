Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your Tiny Treasures experience

What happens to the kit after the event?

+

After your event, you can either drop the kit off at one of our convenient drop-off locations or coordinate a pick-up at your preferred location. We'll provide you with all the details and options when you book!

How far in advance should I book my experience?

+

We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance for best availability, especially for weekend events and bachelorette parties. For custom themes or rush orders, please contact us as early as possible.

What's the minimum number of guests required?

+

Minimum guest requirements vary by experience: 6 guests for bachelorette/girls' night experiences, 4 guests for beaded bag charm workshops, and 6 kids for Little Makers workshops. Contact us for smaller groups - we may be able to accommodate!

How long does each experience take?

+

Most experiences take 1.5-2.5 hours. Kids' workshops are typically 60-90 minutes, while full charm bar experiences can take up to 3 hours depending on the group size and complexity of designs.

Do you provide setup and cleanup?

+

Yes! We handle complete setup and cleanup. Your kits arrive organized and ready to use, and we'll coordinate pickup or provide cleanup instructions for shipped kits.

What if someone has never made jewelry before?

+

Perfect! Our experiences are designed for all skill levels. We provide step-by-step guides with photos, and our kits include everything needed to create beautiful pieces - no prior experience required!

Can I customize the experience for my event theme?

+

Yes! We love creating custom experiences. We can match your color scheme, provide themed charms, and even create custom packaging. Contact us to discuss your vision!

What's your cancellation policy?

+

We offer full refunds for cancellations made 7+ days before your event. Cancellations within 7 days may be subject to a restocking fee. Weather-related cancellations for local delivery are fully refundable.

How do I get my Kit?

+

We provide central pick-up for your kit within Madison. We may also be able to provide local delivery within the Madison, WI area. Contact us to discuss locations outside of Madison.

Can guests take home extra supplies?

+

Each kit includes enough materials for the planned projects plus some extras for creativity. Additional supplies and take-home kits are available for purchase by request for those who wish to continue crafting at home!
